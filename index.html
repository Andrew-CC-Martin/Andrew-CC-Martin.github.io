About me

Hi there! I’m Andrew, a full-stack web developer in training. My background is in applied science; I majored in Food Science and Technology, and have spent my career so far working in quality assurance in the food and medical industries. Away from the computer, I’m very into cooking, especially Asian food, and I also do a little bit of gardening to supply ingredients. I like outdoor/adventure activities such as canyoning, rock climbing, and hiking, and I’m also quite into street unicycling (yes, it’s a thing!). At more sedate times I enjoy yoga, meditation, reading, puzzles, and cheesy Korean dramas.
